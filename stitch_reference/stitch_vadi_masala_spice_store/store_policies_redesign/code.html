Shipping & Delivery

We strive to deliver your spices fresh and fast. All orders are processed within 1-2 business days. You will receive a confirmation email with tracking information once your order has shipped.

Standard Delivery Rates

  • Domestic (Standard 3-5 days) $5.99
  • Expedited (1-2 days) $12.99
  • Orders over $50 check_circle FREE

International Shipping

Currently, we ship to select international destinations. Shipping times and costs vary depending on the location. Duties and taxes are the responsibility of the customer.

Return Policy

Your satisfaction is our priority. Due to the perishable nature of our products, we have a specific return policy to ensure quality and safety.

check_circle What is eligible?

Unopened, sealed packages within 14 days of delivery. Incorrect items received or products damaged during transit.

cancel What is not eligible?

Opened packages. Returns requested after 14 days. Items bought on final sale.

How to initiate a return

  1. Contact our support team within 14 days of receiving your order.
  2. Provide your order number and the reason for return.
  3. We will provide a return shipping label if the return is due to our error.
  4. Refunds will be processed to the original payment method within 5-7 business days after receiving the returned item.

Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This brief overview explains how we handle your information.

Data Collection

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, place an order, or contact customer support. This includes name, email, shipping address, and payment details.

How We Use Your Data

  • To process and fulfill your orders.
  • To communicate with you about your order or our products.
  • To improve our website and services.
  • To protect against fraud.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We only share it with trusted service providers necessary to operate our business (e.g., shipping partners, payment processors).
